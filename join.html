Ready To Join Us?

We're so glad you're ready to join! Here's the steps needed for you to become an official member of ROCC!


1. Fill out the google form from the icon

2. Sign in to getInvolved and click "Join Club". A link is provided in the form.

3. Join the GroupMe or Discord to interact with other members and to stay up-to-date with club events!
